About Institute

Religious institute based on approach of Moderation, unique & innovative scientific curricula, with cadre of teachers, scholars, and educational sheikhs.

Hosting Senior Scholars & Sheikhs

Alerth Alnabawi For Development education councils & circles are concerned with four objectives of education circles, which are:

  • Instilling the greatness of religion in the hearts of learners.
  • Conveying information to their minds and educating them in religion.
  • Imbuing them with noble Islamic morals.
  • Attach their hearts to the call to Allah and carry the burden of religion and the message of Muhammad.

Our curriculum is the curriculum designated for the primary stage followed by Dar Al-Mustafa for Islamic Studies in the singing education.

Institute & Establishment
